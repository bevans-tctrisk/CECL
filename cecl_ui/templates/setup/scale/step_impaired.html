Impaired Loans (ASC 310-10)

Upload the credit union's completed CECL-SCALE Impaired Loans.xlsx file. The data-entry rows (columns A–J on the  Impaired Loans ASC 310-10 tab) are copied into the SCALE output workbook after the 5300 fill. The template's calculation columns (K–Q) and summary block are left untouched and will recompute automatically.

Pool names and impairment types are standardized across all credit unions for SCALE, so no per-CU mapping is required.
