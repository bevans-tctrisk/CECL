Original Credit Score Baseline (optional)

Some loan-data extracts — most commonly VISA / credit-card files — don't carry the member's original credit score. If you've already captured those baseline scores elsewhere (e.g. by hand on the WARM workbook), upload a one-time spreadsheet here and the importer will use it to fill in original_fico_score for any loan whose extract is missing that field.

The file needs a header row with at least Member # and Original FICO. A Loan Suffix column is optional — if you provide one, the lookup is tightened to the specific loan; otherwise every loan that member has will inherit the same baseline score.
